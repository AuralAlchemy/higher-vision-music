Curious about how to submit, get playlisted, or what happens next? Here's everything you need to know.

Pick your submission type — playlist or label — then upload your track or share your link. We'll listen closely and reach out if your sound fits our vibe. No barriers, just honest feedback and support for every artist.

We're all about genre-blending, high-vibe music: lofi, chillhop, ambient, dub, and more. If your track is unique and well-produced, we want to hear it. Unsure if you fit? Submit anyway — we listen to everything.
